## 1\. The reviewer-reliability finding, extended (and a distinct failure mode)

The consolidation (Part V) recorded the first-phase pattern: the reviewer (Claude), acting as a hostile-by-design referee, repeatedly read a coordinate/parametrization artifact as a structural obstruction (R-claude-1 through -5), each caught by the author pointing at the specific artifact, each correction independently checkable and held. The finding there: a hostile reviewer generates *false negatives* by the same mechanism a framework-builder generates *false positives* — mistaking a coordinate feature for a structural one, opposite sign.

  

**The second phase added more of the same species, and one new species worth naming.**

  

Same species (coordinate/relative-information errors, author-corrected, verified):

  

  - **The deflation scare:** the reviewer read "the recursion is generic over degree-1 homogeneous metrics" as deflationary (the framework's content is generic, hence not content). This was a *metric-relative-information* error — the same one the author had already corrected once (cycle-only surgeries are content-bearing while transmitting no signal to the primal boundary). Retracted; the genericity is covariance, not deflation.
  - **The "descriptive bulk" scope cut:** the reviewer privileged the primal's boundary observable as the seat of content and called the bulk descriptive. Retracted: each Petz metric in the bulk is itself a qualified space, so the bulk is a content-bearing family.
  - **The implicit Markovian requirement:** the reviewer promoted a property of the DHO dynamical example (the memory kernel) to a framework requirement. Retracted: the cascade is structurally memoryless, and Markovianity is orthogonal to the cascade's associativity axis, so no load is borne.

  

**The new species — "a test that cannot fail is not evidence" (the more instructive one).** Reaching for the non-abelian holonomy (O-4), the reviewer produced *two* failed constructions before the faithful one:

  

  - a **trivial-cancellation** quantity (a weighted structure-constant ratio whose normalization cancels by construction, so it is invariant for *any* input — it cannot fail); and
  - a **flat connection** built from hand-placed structure constants (zero curvature, so its holonomy is trivially unchanged by anything — it detects nothing).

  

Both would have read as "non-abelian invariance confirmed" and *extended the clean-positive streak falsely*. This is distinct from the coordinate-artifact mode: not "I misread a coordinate" but "I built a test incapable of producing a negative." It is more dangerous, because a test that cannot fail looks identical to a test that passed — from the outside *and* from the inside — until you ask whether it *could* have failed. The reviewer caught both only by checking that the tested object was nontrivial (verifying the connection was non-flat: 120/216 nonzero curvature components) before trusting the result.

  

**The combined finding, stated for reuse.** Across both phases, the reviewer's failures clustered into two named modes:

  

1.  **Coordinate/relative-information artifacts** — reading a gauge feature, or a one-observable-relative absence of signal, as a structural fact. Defended against by coordinate-invariant quantities and by remembering information is metric-relative.
2.  **Unfalsifiable tests** — building a quantity that cannot produce a negative (trivial cancellation, or a trivial/flat object). Defended against by confirming the tested object is nontrivial and the test *can* fail before trusting a "preserved" result.

  

The methods supplement is built around defending against both, plus the d=3 trap. The reliability point that compresses away if not recorded: **the reviewer's "failures" and "successes" were generated by the same machinery the framework is accused of, and the only reliable defense — for either party — was routing disputes to coordinate-invariant computations that could genuinely come out either way.** The discriminator that the reviewer was refereeing rather than confirming, throughout: the reviewer conceded the author's *corrections* (every one checkable and correct) while *holding the line on the author's conclusions* — granting only the math-supported parts, and reporting results *against* its own momentum when the computation cut that way (the interior metric-dependence against the clean-closure drift; non-attraction against its own enthusiasm; the Petz bijection's interior-vs-boundary split against a tidier story). A confirming reviewer folds on conclusions; this one folded on artifacts and held on substance.

  

## 2\. The single bottom-line judgment

Stated once, plainly, separated from the technical detail so it survives if the rest is forgotten. This is the honest net assessment of the entire investigation.

  

**There is a real, narrow, correct mathematical result here, and it is separable from the contested physical edifice.**

  

The defensible core — true at the level of the mathematics of monotone metric spaces *as such*, requiring none of the framework's contested physical claims:

  

**The scale/shape orthogonal decomposition of monotone metric spaces under spectral rescaling.** A density operator's geometry decomposes into a spectral budget (the boundary, metric-independent), a **scale axis** (the cascade peel — a finite tower of spectral renormalizations, which is an **automorphism (bijection) of the full Petz family** of monotone metrics, preserving the complete curved non-abelian fiber geometry up to a scale factor u = 1/G²), and a **shape axis** (the metric label itself, living on the scale-invariant eigenvalue ratios). The two bulk axes are **orthogonal and commute** (verified at the metric-tensor level), because the metric label depends only on the ratios while the peel acts only on the scale. Off the associative limit, a one-parameter deformation produces a **frame-dragging regime** — a linear scale–shape cross-term and O(t) non-commutativity of the tower's rungs, the Kerr-like signature. And the natural foundational orientation is **Petz-first**: the multiplicity of monotone metrics is the cornerstone (nontrivial at d≥3), and Chentsov uniqueness is its degenerate d≤2 terminus, not the foundation.

  

This is correct, it is narrow, and as far as I can determine it is not stated this cleanly in the standard information-geometry literature. It is what should be extracted if anything is.

  

**Everything else is the suggestive periphery, whose realization is the standing instantiation question.** The self-dual points (x² = 1/2, c/√2), the horizon identities, the Heisenberg-conjugate and frame-dragging *physical* readings, the recurring rank-2 motif beyond its two independently-established instances — these are structural resonances and analogies, several of them genuine and clean *as mathematics*, but their status as claims about *physical systems* depends on a step the framework has not closed: **which monotone metric, and which budget identification, a given physical system realizes.** The Petz-first reorientation sharpens this into the framework's single most fundamental open question (O-8): with multiplicity as foundational, the selection of the physical metric at d≥3 is *not* fixed by the foundations, and that selection is where the genuine open physics lives — not at a seam where the mathematics runs out, but at the question that taking multiplicity- first makes unavoidable.

  

**On the framework's overall character, unchanged across the whole investigation:** it is not crankery and it is not a breakthrough. It is a methodologically exemplary, mathematically competent reorganization whose genuine verified content is modest (the decomposition above; the purity speed limit as a corollary with Anandan–Aharonov for saturation; the DHO N=4 quartic and G-distortion identity; the cascade automorphism), whose grander structural theses are mostly false-in-strong-form or open (the Hodge identification, the Grassmannian transitions, the universal-scope-via-Petz claim, the rank-2 curvature, the all-orders anomaly), and whose genuinely unusual virtue is **epistemic discipline** — the program commissions and preserves hostile reviews, tiers its claims, records its own falsifications with numbers, and concedes cleanly. That discipline is the strongest reason to take it seriously, and it is the thing that made this investigation possible: a framework that did not keep its own retraction records could not have been reviewed this way.
