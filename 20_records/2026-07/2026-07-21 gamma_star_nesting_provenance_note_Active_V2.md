## 1. The category distinction

Priority is a two-party relation over the same object. What the corpus holds is **provenance**: dated origination of constructs that appear (see §2) to be uncontested. Two consequences: (a) "no priority weight" in the closure record was scoped to the Liu endpoint quantities, where a contest existed and was lost; it does not apply to γ* or the mechanism, where no contest exists; (b) the operative weakness is not priority but **anchoring** — the publicly defensible date for both objects is currently 2026-07-16 (the repo's single import commit, which contains the V2 reference and the chain); everything earlier rests on account-gated Drive timestamps, per the spine trace's own caveat.
